# Privacy Policy  
**QiPraxis**

_Last updated: March 2026_

QiPraxis ("the App") is a digital breathing and practice support tool designed to assist users in structured breathing exercises and personal practice routines.

This Privacy Policy explains how the application handles information when you use the App.

---

# 1. Information Collection

QiPraxis does **not collect personal information from users**.

You are **not required to create an account** or provide personal details such as:

- Name  
- Email address  
- Phone number  
- Location information  
- Personal identification data  

The application is designed to operate **entirely on your device**.

QiPraxis does **not store or transmit personal data to external servers**.

---

# 2. Local Data Storage

To support normal application functionality, some data may be stored **locally on your device only**.

This may include:

- Practice configuration settings  
- Reminder schedules  
- Audio preferences  
- Application settings  

This information **remains on your device and is not transmitted outside the app**.

---

# 3. Purchases

The application may offer **optional premium content** that can be unlocked through **In-App Purchases**.

All purchases are processed securely through **Google Play Billing**.

QiPraxis:

- does **not receive or store payment information**
- does **not process credit card data**
- does **not have access to your financial details**

Payment transactions are handled entirely by **Google Play** in accordance with Google's payment policies.

---

# 4. Notifications and Reminders

The application may provide **local reminders** to help users maintain regular practice schedules.

These reminders:

- are generated locally on your device  
- do not transmit data to external servers  
- do not track user activity  

Notification settings can be adjusted or disabled within the application or in your device settings.

---

# 5. Data Usage

QiPraxis **does not track or analyze user behavior**.

The application does **not collect information about**:

- how often you practice  
- which exercises you use  
- emotional or mental state  
- personal wellness data  
- activity inside the application  

The app functions as a **self-directed practice tool** and does not attempt to evaluate or influence user behavior.

---

# 6. Third-Party Services

QiPraxis does **not integrate advertising networks or behavioral tracking services**.

However, the application may rely on **Google Play Services**, which are provided by the Android platform.

Google Play Services may collect limited device information as part of the Android operating system.  
These services operate under Google's own privacy policies.

QiPraxis does **not control or access the data collected by Google Play Services**.

---

# 7. External Links

The application may contain links to **external resources**, such as reference materials or informational websites.

If you follow these links, you will be directed to websites that are **not operated by QiPraxis**.

We recommend reviewing the **privacy policies of those external sites** before providing any personal information.

---

# 8. Health and Wellness Disclaimer

QiPraxis is a **practice support application** intended for breathing exercises and personal training routines.

The application **does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment**.

QiPraxis should **not be used as a substitute for professional medical guidance**.

If you have medical concerns, please consult a qualified healthcare professional.

---

# 9. Children's Privacy

QiPraxis does **not knowingly collect personal information from anyone**, including children under the age of 13.

The application is **not specifically directed toward children**, and because no personal data is collected, no personal information from children is processed.

---

# 10. Security

Because QiPraxis does not collect or transmit personal information, user activity primarily remains under the control of the device owner.

However, no digital system can guarantee absolute security.  
Users should take standard precautions when using any mobile device.

---

# 11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in the application or legal requirements.

Any updates will be published on the official Privacy Policy page.

---
