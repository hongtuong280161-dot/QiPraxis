# Privacy Policy

**QiPraxis – Khí Công Toàn Thư Số**

QiPraxis built the **Khí Công Toàn Thư Số** application as a breathing and practice support tool.  
This application is provided as a simple practice environment and is intended for use as is.

This page explains our approach to **privacy, data usage, and information collection** when you use the application.

---

## Information Collection

The application **does not collect personal information from users**.

You are not required to create an account or provide personal details such as:

- name  
- email address  
- phone number  
- location information  

The application is designed to function **entirely on your device**.

Practice settings and preferences are stored **locally on your device only**.

---

## Data Usage

The application **does not track or analyze user behavior**.

We do not collect information about:

- how often you practice
- how you use breathing exercises
- your emotional or mental state
- your activity within the app

The application does not attempt to evaluate or influence your practice.

---

## Third-Party Services

The application **does not integrate advertising networks or tracking services**.

Some system components provided by the Android platform (such as **Google Play Services**) may operate in the background as part of the operating system. These services are outside the control of the application.

The application itself **does not send personal data to third-party services**.

---

## Local Data Storage

Some information may be stored locally on your device to support normal app functionality. This may include:

- practice configuration settings
- reminder settings
- audio preferences

This data **remains on your device and is not transmitted to external servers**.

---

## Security

Because the application does not collect or transmit personal information, your usage data remains primarily under your own control on your device.

However, no digital system can guarantee absolute security, and users should take normal precautions when using any digital device.

---

## Links to External Sites

The application may contain links to external resources (such as reference materials or sound sources).

If you follow these links, you will be directed to external websites.

These sites are **not operated by QiPraxis**, and we recommend reviewing their privacy policies separately.

---

## Children's Privacy

The application does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Because no personal data is collected, there is no intentional data processing related to children.

---

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in the application or legal requirements.

Any updates will be published on the official Privacy Policy page.

---

## Effective Date

This policy is effective for **version 1.0 and later** of the application.
