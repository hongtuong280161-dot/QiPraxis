# Medical Disclaimer  
**QiPraxis**

QiPraxis is a breathing and personal practice support application.

The application is **not a medical device** and does not provide medical advice.

---

## Not Medical Advice

The exercises and content provided in the app are intended for **general wellness and personal practice**.

They should not be considered:

- medical advice
- medical diagnosis
- medical treatment

---

## Health Conditions

If you have medical conditions such as:

- respiratory issues
- cardiovascular conditions
- dizziness or fainting
- other health concerns

please consult a qualified healthcare professional before practicing breathing exercises.

---

## Personal Responsibility

Users practice exercises **at their own discretion and responsibility**.

If you feel discomfort during practice, stop immediately.

---

## Emergency Situations

QiPraxis should **not be used in medical emergencies**.

Always seek professional medical help if needed.
